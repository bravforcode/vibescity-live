Create `src/components/ui/ConsentBanner.vue` — a one-time PDPA consent overlay shown on first visit.

**Component behavior:**
- Renders as a fixed bottom banner (z-index: 950, above action sheet at 900) with dark glass-morphism background matching VibeActionSheet style (rgba(10,10,20,0.95), backdrop-filter: blur(12px)).
- Shows a concise message: "We use cookies and local storage to save your game progress and improve your experience." (via `t('consent.message')`).

- Component self-hides: use a reactive `showBanner` ref initialized from `!localStorage.getItem("pdpa_consent_ts")`. After accept, set to false.
- All visible strings use `t()` from vue-i18n (import `useI18n`).
- Touch target: Accept button min 44px height, full-width.
- Reduced motion: wrap transition in `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` to disable.
