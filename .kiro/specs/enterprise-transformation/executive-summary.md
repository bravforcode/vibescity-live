## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigation

### Critical Risks

#### 1. Database Migration Complexity
**Risk Level:** High  
**Impact:** Service disruption, data loss  
**Mitigation:**
- Extensive testing in staging
- Gradual rollout with rollback plan
- 24/7 monitoring during migration
- Database backup and recovery procedures

**Contingency:** Immediate rollback to previous state

#### 2. Team Bandwidth
**Risk Level:** Medium  
**Impact:** Timeline delays  
**Mitigation:**
- Clear prioritization
- External contractors if needed
- Realistic sprint planning
- Regular progress reviews

**Contingency:** Extend timeline or reduce scope

#### 3. Third-Party Dependencies
**Risk Level:** Medium  
**Impact:** Integration delays  
**Mitigation:**
- Vendor evaluation and selection
- Fallback options identified
- POCs before commitment
- Contract SLAs

**Contingency:** Switch to alternative vendors
