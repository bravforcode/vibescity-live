## Goal

- Build a production-safe source-hunt queue for every venue that still lacks complete authoritative media.
- Keep the queue grounded in the live authoritative media index instead of stale CSV snapshots.

## Outputs

1. A machine-readable CSV for all incomplete venues.
2. A concise decision memo grouping the queue by research lane and expected yield.

## Rules

- Do not mark a venue complete unless both real image and real video are verifiably owned or authority-backed.
- Prefer owned websites, official social posts/reels/videos, and authority directories.
- Keep low-yield branch and institutional tails hidden until direct proof exists.

## Success Criteria

- Queue reflects the current live gap count.
- Each row includes a recommended research lane and concrete search query seed.
- The memo states which groups are worth continued hunting and which should stay hidden.
